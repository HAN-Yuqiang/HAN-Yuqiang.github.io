About Me

Yuqiang Han(韩玉强) is currently a second-year Ph.D. student from the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University(ZJU), supervised by Prof. Jian Wu. He received his B.E. degree from the Software College, Shandong University(SDU) in 2015. His research interests are machine learning and data mining.
